pybedtools.contrib.MultiClassifier.classify¶

MultiClassifier.classify(**kwargs)¶

Classify the features in self.bed, populating several dictionaries in self. kwargs are passed on to BedTool.multi_intersect. The "empty=True" kwarg to multi_intersect is always provided to make sure the classification works correctly.
